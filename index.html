OUR CAPABILITIES

Int'l Precision Assemblies Inc. is an EMS provider with seamless Manufacturing Solution providing Engineering Design, Original Equipment Manufacturing, Original Design Manufacturing and Supply Chain Management. We deliver fast-time to market, committed to highest level of product quality and flexible business model for our Customers

  • We have four plants for manufacturing solutions, engineering and design center in Philippines.
  • We are dedicated to provide manufacturing excellence and expertise in design and Engineering.
  • We are capable of low-volume, high-mix (LVHM), and high-volume, low-mix (HVLM) Manufacturing.
  • We have the most competent, innovative and highly-skilled Engineers.

We have been in operation since March 2003 creating uninterruptible power supply (UPS), replacement battery cartridges, and battery modules for American Power Conversion (APC Brand of Schneider Electric); surge arresters for Powertech Technology Inc. (www.pti.com.tw); 3D printers for Global 3D Systems Inc. (www. https://www.g3dsys.com), while also offering printed circuit board assembly services for products like 2-way radios, TV antennas, mobile phones, and LED lights. Two-way radio for Motorola (www.motorolasolutions.com).
